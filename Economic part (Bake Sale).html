Economic part (Bake Sale)

The idea of the Bake sale is that we bake goods, and we put them on a table to sell them. I got the idea of the Bake sale when in class we divided tasks and I got the environmental one, so I thought about ways to make money, and I thought Bake Sale. I’ve always loved baking goods so that might have inspired me. When the day of the Bake Sale happened, we brought Rice Crispy Treats, Graham Cracker Brownie’s, Special Cakes, Juices, crepes, cookies, lemonade, and Macaroons. We hope to get m30€ minimum and send it to the organization Earth Day Network.
